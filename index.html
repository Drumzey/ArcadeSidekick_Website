    Every hero needs a sidekick!

    Welcome to Arcade Sidekick!

    Never forget your highscores again. Record your top scores on arcade classics, add friends and compare your best on our leaderboards. Go head to head against other gamers to determine who has bragging rights as the best retro gamer.

    Join our mission to help promote and encourage the preservation and competition of old arcade games.

  • Features

    Look at all this loot! There is so much more to come and many new levels to reach.

    Main Menu

    Navigate round the app from our retro menu.

    Friends and Enemies

    Browse the Sidekick community. Select your friends to appear on your leaderboards. Exclude your enemies, anyone you think doesn’t play fair will not appear on your leaderboards.

    My Scores

    See your top scores for each game you’ve played listed here and see quickly which ones your rivals have posted scores for too.

    Games

    Browse the entire catalog in all games (it might take a while), or select the genre your looking for (we’ve done our best to categorise each game but it hasn’t been easy!)

    Head To Head

    Compare yourself head to head with a friend, or compare two of your friends to see who is the best gamer statistically. Maths is fact!

    Top 50

    See the top 50 games as rated by the community. Don’t like what you see go vote for your favourite!

    Recent Activity

    See the last 100 events in the system from all users, the scores posted and the users who have joined.

    Friend Activity

    Check your friends updates since you last logged in, will display new and updated scores.

    Game

    Learn a little about your favourite games, see the original artwork (where available), record your scores and keep your notes under each game. View ratings and share your scores on social media.

    Leaderboards

    Browse the friends, global and club leaderboards to compare yourself to your rivals.

    Themes

    Chose from our retro inspired themes and please let us know of any others you might like!

  • Coming Soon

    This is just some of what we are currently working on. Release dates TBC, but soon...

    Detailed Score Submissions

    You will soon be able to store a more detailed score submission including location, settings and date information. Along side this each leaderboard will contain the ability to switch settings, so you can see who has used the exact same game settings as you. This will enable you to submit scores for individual levels, so scoring of rhythm action and dance games will now be possible!

    Venues

    Want to learn about arcade venues near you? Hand in hand with the detailed score submissions is the ability to see and select the venues that you play to make them quickly available in the score submission section. You will also be able to see which games are available at each location as scores are logged for each. One day i'll create an arcade map where we can see which arcades are available and where in the world you can play a particular game!

    Arcade Quiz!

    Inspired by the two geniuses/genii/biscuit eaters over at Ten Pence Arcade the Arcade Quiz section will generate questions based upon the game catalog, plus a couple of extra ones i've added in. How many will you get right in a row? Keep track and share your scores with the world.

    Vectrex

    Like mini arcade machines in their own right, i've had a number of requests for a Vectrex section in the app. So available soon you will be able register highscore for a small amount of Vectrex games. As always send in any game requests that you have and i'll get them added as soon as I can.

  • Story

    Our goal at Sidekick HQ is to create the app the retro arcade community cannot live without. Recording top scores, creating global leaderboards, giving you arcade news and reviews, teaching you about the best retro gaming podcasts, new venues, competitions, the lot! We have a long way to go but we’ve dropped the difficulty and popped on infinite lives so will get there eventually! The story so far.....

    Level 1 - The Beginning

    My love for the arcades didn’t start at a seaside resort or caravan park, but with the BBC Micro versions and clones of arcade classics. We didn’t play Amidar, we had crazy painter, Donkey Kong? It was Killer Gorilla to us! And no one had heard of defender, it was planetoid to us! Mr Ee! Not Mr Do! I also started coding very young and spent many an hour copying code from the backs of magazines.

    It wasn’t until many years later that I learnt the true origins of many of my favourite games and got to play them at real arcades, and I never looked back.

    Level 2 - The Prototype

    After watching a documentary on collectors/hoarders I learnt of an arcade not too far from where I lived. Haslingdon, the home of the first Arcade Club was just a small venue but I found myself going back again and again. Each time I went I had forgotten my best scores and figured with my background in computing I could do something about it.

    The Sidekick was born and soon I was armed with my own person record of my favourite games and my (mostly pathetic) top scores. I hadn’t considered releasing it until I met with Andy Palmer (Arcade Club) to show him what I had done, and the reception I got from him and his staff was extremely encouraging.

    Level 3 - Release V1.0.0

    After many months of testing, conversations and help from members of the arcade community (Thanks Shaun and Vic from Ten Pence Arcade, Andy from Arcade Club, Pete Hahn from the Galloping Ghost to mention but a few), I had the confidence to click the Submit button and suddenly Sidekick was available for the whole world to see.

    The reception I received was staggering, and I found myself reading the same reviews on the app stores over and over again. My little app had 5 stars reviews, I had people emailing me to say thankyou for releasing an app they had been after for years, and others offering their help and assistance in the future development of the app, sending me artwork and game information for games not yet in the catalogue.

    Level 4 - Feedaback and new features

    After release I got a lot of requests for new games and new features. From the initial catalogue of 250 games, we have now grown to over 500. We now have global leader boards, a recent activity feed and friend update tracker, a way to compare yourself head to head with other gamers and add your own custom games.

    I couldn’t have done this without the help of the incredible retro gaming community. Thankyou to everyone who has retweeted my posts, shared their scores online or recommended the app to their friends. A massive thankyou to the Ten Pence Arcade guys for talking about the app on numerous episode of there excellent podcast, im sure most of my user base are their listeners!

    Level 5 - Future

    Well I have a lot of plans for the app and website in the future. Firstly, I want to be able to help you track where you got your scores and what the games settings were. This will make the leader boards more accurate and will give me the ability to provide high scores per venue. I want to set up gaming leagues in the clubs feature, so you can start your own mini leagues and competitions. We’ll be adding photo uploads for those that demand evidence and enhancing the artwork and game information where we can, providing playthrough video footage and hints and tips to hep master each game.

    Keep your eye on the releases and please get in touch if you have further ideas or feedback.
